## Program Structure Summary

### Eligibility Pathways (3)
1. **RI Works CCAP** - TANF/RIW cash recipients with approved employment plan
2. **Income Eligible** - Working families below 261% FPL, 20+ hrs/week
3. **College Program** - Students at CCRI/RIC/URI with 7+ credits or 20+ combined hrs

### Income Eligibility Tests
1. **Gross income test:** Countable income <= 261% FPL (entry) or <= 300% FPL (transitional)
2. **Asset test:** Liquid assets <= $1,000,000
3. **Minimum activity:** 20 hours/week work, education, or training (each parent in 2-parent home)
4. **Minimum wage:** Earned income >= greater of state or federal minimum wage per hour

### Income Definition (Section 4.2, Definition 30/29)
Countable income includes: wages/salary/commissions/tips/bonuses, self-employment AGI, RSDI, SSI, dividends/interest, estate/trust income, rental income, room/board income, public assistance, unemployment, TDI, workers' comp, pensions/annuities, health insurance cash-outs, adoption subsidies, alimony, child support, regular contributions from non-household persons, royalties, strike benefits, trade readjustment allowance, VA compensation/benefits, Americorps payments (not VISTA), foster care (when child in unit), in-kind assistance, non-citizen sponsor income.

### Excluded Income (Section 4.2, Definition 22)
26 categories excluded: USDA donated foods, relocation payments (42 USC 4601-4655), educational grants/loans, Indian tribe per-capita payments, elderly nutrition (Older Americans Act), foster grandparent/SCORE/ACE volunteer payments, child nutrition supplements, experimental housing allowance, Indian tribe receipts (PL 94-114), Alaska Native Claims payments, foster care (child NOT in unit), food assistance, government housing subsidies, home energy assistance, college work-study, dependent child earned income, WIOA stipends/income, EITC refunds/advance payments, loans/grants for non-living costs, SSA PASS/IRWE, teen parent's parents' income, Section 8 utility payments, veterans aid/attendant allowances, Americorps/VISTA volunteer payments, RI Works cash assistance, veterans disability pension.

### Copayment (Family Share) Structure
Source: 218-RICR-20-00-4.6.1(C)

| Level | FPL Range | % of Gross Income |
|-------|-----------|-------------------|
| 0 | <= 100% FPL | 0% (no family share) |
| 1 | > 100% to <= 125% FPL | 2% |
| 2 | > 125% to <= 150% FPL | 5% |
| 3 | > 150% to <= 261% FPL | 7% |
| 4 | > 261% to <= 300% FPL (transitional only) | 7% |

Special rules:
- RI Works recipients: $0 copayment (Section 4.5.4)
- Housing insecure families: $0 copayment (Section 4.5.4(B))
- Single copayment regardless of number of children
- Assigned to youngest eligible child enrolled in care

### Provider Rate Structure
Rates vary by 3 dimensions:
1. **Provider type:** Licensed Center, Licensed Family Child Care, Licensed Exempt
2. **Child age:** Infant (6wk-18mo), Toddler (18mo-3yr), Preschool (3yr-1st grade), School Age (1st grade-13yr)
   - Note: Family child care combines Infant/Toddler
3. **Time authorization:** Full-time (30+ hrs), Three-quarter (20-29 hrs), Half-time (10-19 hrs), Quarter-time (<10 hrs)
4. **QRIS BrightStars rating:** Star 1-5 (centers/family) or Step 1-4 (exempt)

### Benefit Calculation
**Formula:** Benefit = Provider Weekly Rate - Family Share (copayment)
- Provider is paid the lesser of: their actual charge OR the DHS established rate for their provider type/age/time/rating
- Family share = percentage of gross countable income (per copayment table)
- Family share is a single amount regardless of number of children
- Family share assigned to youngest child (highest rate child)
- If family share exceeds rate for youngest child, remainder distributed among other providers

### Child Age Categories (Section 4.12(B))
- **Infant/Toddler:** 6 weeks up to 3 years
- **Preschool:** 3 years up to 1st grade entry (includes ALL kindergarten)
- **School Age:** 1st grade up to 13 years

### Certification Period
- Minimum 24 months from authorization date
- Redetermination required at end of certification period

### Childcare Worker Pilot Program (218-RICR-20-00-13)
- Separate regulation: https://rules.sos.ri.gov/regulations/Part/218-20-00-13
- Childcare workers: $0 copayment if 20+ hrs/week, income up to 300% FPL
- Active through July 31, 2028

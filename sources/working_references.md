## SSA State Assistance Programs for SSI Recipients, January 2011 — Alaska

**Source**: https://www.ssa.gov/policy/docs/progdesc/ssi_st_asst/2011/ak.pdf (downloaded by user)

### Program Overview (2011)
- Administration: Department of Health and Social Services, Division of Public Assistance
- Effective date: January 1, 1974
- Statutory basis: Alaska Statutes 47.25.430-47.25.615
- State-administered optional supplementation
- Eligibility: SSI program guidelines (Title XVI), state-determined
- Resource limitations: Federal SSI regulations apply
- Income exclusions: Federal SSI regulations apply; in-kind income also excluded
- Children under age 18 not eligible
- Excludes: Alaska Pioneer Homes residents, nonmedical public institutions, institutions for mental disorders
- Payment calculation: State standard minus federal SSI payment minus countable income = state supplement
- For non-SSI recipients: State standard minus countable income
- Total expenditures: $55,946,103 for calendar year 2010
- Number of recipients: 17,630 (January 2011)

### Definitions
- **Living independently**: Includes persons who live alone, with minor child/spouse/deemor, in household where all receive public assistance, or in household of another paying prorated share or having ownership interest.
- **Living in the household of another**: Persons residing in another's household for full calendar month receiving both food and shelter.
- **Assisted living home**: Persons residing in an assisted living home for a full calendar month.
- **Medicaid facility**: Recipients in skilled nursing or intermediate care facility certified by Alaska DHSS.
